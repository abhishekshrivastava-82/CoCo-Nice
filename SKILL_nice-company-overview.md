# NICE Ltd – Company Overview

## Introduction

NICE Ltd is a global enterprise software company focused on AI-driven customer experience (CX), automation, and financial crime prevention.

It provides cloud-native platforms that connect people, data, and workflows to enable intelligent, automated decision-making across enterprises.

NICE operates in over 150 countries and supports thousands of organizations in scaling AI-driven customer interactions.

---

## What NICE Does

NICE builds AI-powered platforms that:

- Automate customer interactions
- Augment human workforce with AI
- Enable end-to-end customer journey orchestration
- Deliver real-time insights and decisioning

---

## Core Business Segments

### 1. Customer Experience (CX)

- Platform: CXone
- Capabilities:
  - Omnichannel customer engagement
  - AI-powered routing and automation
  - Workforce engagement management
  - Conversational AI & virtual agents

### 2. Financial Crime & Compliance

- Platform: NICE Actimize
- Capabilities:
  - Anti-money laundering (AML)
  - Fraud detection
  - Risk analytics
  - Regulatory compliance
